About This: Data Quality

Data quality ensures that data is fit for its intended purpose. This includes monitoring and improving data accuracy, completeness, consistency, timeliness, and validity across all systems and processes.

High-quality data is the foundation of reliable analytics, effective decision-making, and successful business operations. Poor data quality can lead to costly mistakes and missed opportunities.

Data Quality Framework

Focus Areas

Data Validation

Implementing validation rules, constraints, and checks at ingestion and transformation stages. Schema validation, type checking, and range validation.

Quality Metrics

Measuring data quality across dimensions: accuracy, completeness, consistency, timeliness, validity, and uniqueness. Establishing SLAs and monitoring quality trends.

Data Profiling

Analyzing data distributions, patterns, and anomalies. Statistical profiling, outlier detection, and understanding data characteristics for quality assessment.

Quality Tools

Using tools like Great Expectations, Soda, dbt tests, and custom validation frameworks. Automated testing, alerting, and quality reporting.
