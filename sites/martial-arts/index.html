About This: Martial Arts Training

This space chronicles my martial arts journey mostly as a solo practitioner. Without regular access to a dojo or training partners, I've developed a structured approach to solo training that emphasizes fundamentals, progressive skill development, and measurable improvement.

Solo training presents unique challenges but also unique opportunities—the freedom to explore different styles, focus deeply on technique, and develop a highly personalized training regimen that fits my schedule and goals.

Current Training Focus

Striking Fundamentals

Building a solid foundation in punches, kicks, elbows, and knees. Focus on proper form, power generation through hip rotation, and maintaining balance throughout combinations.

Footwork & Movement

Developing agile footwork patterns, stance transitions, and spatial awareness. Working on angles, pivots, and maintaining proper distance in shadow boxing drills.

Solo Drills

Creating and refining solo training drills that simulate partner work—shadow boxing, heavy bag work, speed bag training, and visualization exercises.

Conditioning

Building martial arts-specific conditioning through circuit training, plyometrics, core work, and cardiovascular endurance tailored to combat sports.
