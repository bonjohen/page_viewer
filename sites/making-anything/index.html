Making Anything

"I will eventually be able to build anything"

For over 15 years, I've been building the capability to create anything I can imagine. This space documents my maker journey—from digital fabrication to traditional crafts, electronics to chemistry, software to physical objects.

The philosophy is simple: acquire tools, learn techniques, build projects, and expand what's possible. Every new capability unlocks new creative possibilities.
